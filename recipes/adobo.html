Filipino Chicken Adobo Recipe

Back to Homepage

Chicken Adobo is one of the most famous dish in the Philippines. It is delicious and juicy and pairs good with hot rice.

Don't worry! I will show you how to make Adobo. But first let's gather the ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Bay Leaves
  • Garlic
  • Green Onion
  • Pepper corns
  • Sugar
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
    Tips:
  • boneless skinless chicken thighs - cannot substitute with breast. We need the fat to transform sauce into a glaze.
  • soy sauce - all purpose soy sauce.
  • white vinegar - Datu Puti or any day to day vinegar

How to make it?

Steps on how to make Chicken Adobo:

  1. Combine the Chicken Thigh, soy sauce and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour. (For better taste.)
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated Chicken Thigh. Cook this all up for a few minutes.
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Add salt and sugar to taste.
  6. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy! (Don't forget the rice!)
